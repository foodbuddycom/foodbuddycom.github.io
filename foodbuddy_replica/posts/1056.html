I love to have onion in my meals, yes, it might smell awkward 😖 but, in my opinion, it gives a great taste to any meal😋. An interesting fact🤓, onions contain over 100 sulfide-containing compounds. While they don’t all have the same level of health impact 👊🏼, they all have similar health benefits👍🏼 so don't be afraid of them 😉
