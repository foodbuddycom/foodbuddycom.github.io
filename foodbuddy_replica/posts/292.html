Another delicious breakfast recipe for you 😋 I was always unhappy with granola that’s sold in the store so I made my own and it’s awesome 🤤 best thing is that I can select my own ingredients and have total control over granola quality 👌🏼 ✅200 g rolled oats 🥜150 g mixed nuts (any nuts you have: almonds, pecan, walnuts, hazelnuts cashews etc.) 🌻 50 g mixed seeds (any seeds you have: pumpkin, sunflower, chia, flax, poppy, sesame etc.) 🥄1 tsp cinnamon 🍯 5 tbsp runny honey 🥄 5 tbsp olive oil 🍇 100 g dried fruits (raisins, prunes, dried apricots, cranberry etc.) Mix all ingredients except dried fruits and bake in the oven at 150 C/350 F for 30 min, stir every 5-10 min.
