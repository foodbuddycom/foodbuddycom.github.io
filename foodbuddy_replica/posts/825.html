Power breakfast before 21 K trail running with 600 m elevation 💪🏼 Apple and berries compote🍎🍓🍇, with plain yogurt and mix of nuts, oats, raisins and manuka honey 🍯 healthy and easy to make breakfast - boil for 2-3 min (or until apples are soft) strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, raspberries (or whatever berries are available) and apples in 1/2 cup of water 👍🏼add 1 tsp of sugar if berries are too sour. In the meanwhile roast or bake mix of nuts, seeds, oats and raisins (make them hot) and mix with honey. Add layers of compote, yogurt and nuts & honey mix to the bowl and enjoy!
