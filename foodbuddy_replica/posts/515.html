Delicious and light vegan dish - lentils, carrot & zucchini casserole with onion, garlic, fresh thyme and coriander, curry powder, salt and pepper. You will need: 1 big carrot grated, 1 glass of dried lentils cooked in salted hot water, 1 zucchini grated, 1 big onion finely chopped, 2 garlic cloves crushed, 3 spoons of wholemeal flour, 1 egg yolk, 1/2 cup of low fat milk 2 spoons of olive oil, 1 tsp of curry powder, 1 tsp of thyme, 3-4 sprigs of coriander. On a pan with olive oil stir fry onion, garlic till soft, add carrot, add zucchini, salt, pepper, milk - mix well and cook for 5-7 min.
