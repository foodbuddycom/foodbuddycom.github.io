Vegan diet redefined with Yotam Ottolenghi cook book 👍🏼🌿👍🏼🍅👍🏼🍉 today for lunch Tomato and Watermelon Gazpacho! Yummy until the very last drop. Blend blanched, pealed and chopped tomatoes (2kg) with chopped celery (6 stalks, small onion, 5 garlic cloves, watermelon flesh (0.4 kg), bread (4 slices), tomato puree (0.15 kg), basil leaves (15 g), add while blending 2 tbsp of wine vinegar, olive oil (200 ml), 3/4 teaspoon of salt and good grind of black pepper. Top up with croutons (white bread mixed with salt, olive oil and red wine vinegar, baked for 12 min), basil and olive oil and serve! 👌🏼 simple, fresh and healthy! 😀💪🏼 thank you mr. Ottolenghi!
