We’ve had cravings for glass noodles lately, so we cooked them! Cooked them with minced beef, yakiniku & soya sauces, mushrooms, carrots, ginger, onion, sesame and sunflower oil, and rice wine! It won’t take you long to make this delicious meal, so save this recipe: INGREDIENTS: ➖500 g minced beef ➖ 1 onion finely chopped ➖ 1 inch /2.5 cm ginger grated ➖ 1 carrot cut into thin stripes ➖ 150-200 g shiitake and wood ear mushrooms sliced (or any mushrooms of your choice) ➖ 1 tbsp rice wine ➖1 tbsp sesame sauce ➖1 tbsp sunflower oil ➖ 2 tbsp light soya sauce ➖ 2 tbsp yakiniku sauce ➖ ~ 400 g frozen glass noodles COOKING METHOD ➖ pour sunflower oil into a deep frying pan, add onion and ginger and stir fry on high heat until onion is golden. ➖reduce the heat and add minced beef, carrots and mushrooms, cover with a lid and cook for 10–15 min, stirring occasionally ➖ add sauces, rice wine and sesame oil and cook for another 5 min, stirring occasionally ➖ blanch frozen noodles in a hot water until they are soft, add them to minced beef, stir well and cook for few more min. ➖you may want to add salt and pepper to your taste.
