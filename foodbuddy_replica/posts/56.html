Nothing scary, just a hummus recipe 😉: INGREDIENTS: - 1 1/2 can cooked chickpeas - 60 g tahini paste (stirred well) - 50 ml lemon juice - 30 ml extra virgin olive oil - 1/2 tea spoon ground cumin - 1/2 tea spoon salt - 45 ml chilled water - 1 garlic clove minced COOKING METHOD: 1️⃣ pour tahini paste and lemon juice to food processor and blend for 1 min. Scrape the walls of food processor and blend for 30 seconds more. 2️⃣ add garlic, cumin, salt, olive oil and process for another minute. 3️⃣ scrape the walls and add half of chickpeas. Process for 1 min. 4️⃣ add second half of chickpeas and process for one more min. Add chilled water while processing. 5️⃣ check the taste, add salt, or tahini paste if needed. 🎃 Enjoy! 🎃 ___ #halloween #halloween2020 #hummus #homemadehummus #cookathome #healthyrecipes #sgfoodie #foodsg #thrivemags #timeoutsg #food52 #buzzfeedfood #buzzfeedtasty #hummusrecipe #singapore
