Hey guys, want to share a triathlon muffins recipe👍🏻 😄based on whole wheat flour, honey, coconut oil apples and blueberries 👍🏻 you can add sugar on top at the end but I didn't do it and had no regrets 😉 Here you go: 1 and 3/4 cups (of whole wheat flour) 1.5 tsp of baking powder 0.5 tsp of baking soda 0.5 tsp of salt 1 tsp of cinnamon 1/3 cup of coconut oil 1 grated apple 1 apple cut in small cubes 1 pack of blueberries (125 g) 2 eggs 0.5 cup of honey 0.5 cup of yogurt (I used low fat) Bake at 350 F/175 C for 30 min or until muffins cooked (do toothpick check 😉) Share you own #triathlonmuffins under this tag 😁 #triathlon #ironmantri #healthyfood #sportnutrition #running #swimbikerun #marathon #dietfood #cervelo #orbea #specializedbikes #giantbikes #canyonbikes #bmcbikes #boardmanbikes #trailrunning #ironmantraining #marathontraining #muffins
