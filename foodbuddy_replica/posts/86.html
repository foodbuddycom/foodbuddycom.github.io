Mastering our healthy oatmeal-banana-chocolate muffins! Made some changes and like them a lot! Last week we did absolutely yummy muffins with no added sugar and a good thought came after we ate all of them 😋☝🏼why not to add walnuts and replace sunflower oil with coconut oil 🤔 so I did it and muffins became even better 👌🏼 encourage you to test this updated recipe 😉 INGREDIENTS ➖ 1 cup rolled oats blended into flour ➖ 1 cup bread flour ➖ 1/2 cup buttermilk powder ➖ 1 tsp baking soda ➖ 1/2 tsp salt ➖ 1/3 cup coconut oil ➖ 2 ripe bananas ➖ 3/4 cup milk ➖ 1 egg ➖ 1 bar dark chocolate ➖ 2 handfuls walnuts chopped or crushed COOKING METHOD ➖ mix all dry ingredients together ➖ blend bananas ➖ whisk egg, add milk, oil, and blended bananas ➖ add liquids to dry ingredients and mix well until you get a smooth batter. ➖ fill muffin form with batter half way and add some dark chocolate, then add more batter (up to edges of the form (not edges of paper cup) ➖ bake 20-35 min at 205 C/ 400 F ENJOY!!! ___ #muffins #healthysnackideas #healthysnack #healthydesserts #healthytreats #cookathome #onmyplate #darkchocolate #nosugaradded #feedfeed #foodblogfeed #healthyrecipes #buzzfoodfeed #buzzfeedtasty #sgfoodie #foodsg #sgeats
