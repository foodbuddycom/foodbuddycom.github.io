Worth to be saved Chicken Soup recipe 📝🍲👌🏼 1/2 chicken - make a chicken broth. Once chicken is cooked - take it out, cool it down and cut into cubes. In parallel sauté vegetables: 2 big carrots cut into thin 5 cm stripes 🥕 3 medium potatoes cut into cubes 🥔 1 large leek chopped 2 garlic cloves crushed 2 fresh thyme sprigs 1 fresh rosemary sprig Don’t add oil when cooking veggies, use chicken broth instead. Once veggies are semi-cooked, add them to chicken broth together with chicken cubes. Add soba noodles at the end as they will cook very fast. Add salt and pepper to your taste.
