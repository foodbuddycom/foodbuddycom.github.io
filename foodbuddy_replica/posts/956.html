Chicken and veggie stew in Indian style ☺️ Super yummy, healthy, hearty! 🍲👌🏼 Marinate chicken for 30 min in masala- yogurt mix (1 tbsp chicken curry masala, 1/2 cup of low fat yogurt and 4 crushed cloves of garlic, 2 spoons of vegetable oil) Stew with veggies, tomato puree, coconut milk, add more masala paste and salt to your taste - ENJOY!
