Mung beans stole my heart 😍 try to make chicken soup with mung beans and you will understand me. Make chicken stock, cook mung beans, sauté garlic, onion and chilli (seeded) until onions are soft. Add chicken stock to mung beans and bring them to boil, add sautéed garlic, onion and Chilli and simmer for 10-15 min! Add cilantro/coriander, salt & pepper and enjoy 😋🍲 this is my the most favourite soup so far! 💚
