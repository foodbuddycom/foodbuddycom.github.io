This dish has very nice mixture of flavors: sweet and spicy and sour and fresh 😋 mix all ingredients (ex. fennel, curry and coriander leaves) season well with olive oil, salt and pepper and bake for 20 min at 200 degrees C/392 F. Add thinly sliced fennel, curry leaves and bake for another 10 min. Top up with coriander leaves when serving.
