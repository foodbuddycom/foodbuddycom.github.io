THE BEST MUFFINS WE’VE EVER MADE! Starting this long weekend on a sweet positive note 😁 this, kids approved, healthy muffins recipe is a must save one 👍🏼Oatmeal, banana muffins 🍌 with dark chocolate 🍫 core 😋 and no added sugar. INGREDIENTS ➖ 1 cup rolled oats blended into flour ➖ 1 cup bread flour ➖ 1/2 cup buttermilk powder ➖ 1 tsp baking soda ➖ 1/2 tsp salt ➖ 1/3 cup sunflower oil ➖ 2 ripe bananas ➖ 3/4 cup milk ➖ 1 egg ➖ 1 bar dark chocolate COOKING METHOD ➖ mix all dry ingredients together ➖ blend bananas ➖ whisk egg, add milk oil, and blended bananas ➖ add liquids to dry ingredients and mix well until you get a smooth batter. ➖ fill muffin form with batter half way and add some dark chocolate, then add more batter (up to edges of the form (not edges of paper cup) ➖ bake 20-35 min at 205 C/ 400 F ENJOY!!! ___ #muffins #healthysnackideas #healthysnack #healthydesserts #healthytreats #cookathome #onmyplate #darkchocolate #nosugaradded #feedfeed #foodblogfeed #healthyrecipes #buzzfoodfeed #buzzfeedtasty #sgfoodie #foodsg #sgeats
