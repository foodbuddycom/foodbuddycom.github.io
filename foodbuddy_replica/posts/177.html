Kicking off weekend with this #powerbreakfast 👊🏼 - Matcha & coconut oatmeal with seeds, nuts and fruits 🍲 After this breakfast we think you are ready to run to the moon and back 😂 joking of course, BUT this energy bomb will keep you going for very long time OR this can be a great recovery meal after extensive training 👌🏼. This meal is rich in: - Antioxidants (matcha tea powder) - Omega-3 fatty acids (all nuts and seeds) - Potassium (banana) - Healthy carbs and plant-based protein (oats and seeds) - and many other minerals and vitamins! Save this recipe: INGREDIENTS - rolled oats, 1 big cup - 2 tbsp of chia seeds -flaxseeds -hempseed mix - coconut 🥥 water - water - 1 tbsp honey 🍯 - 1 tbsp of matcha powder (can add more if you like the taste) - handful of mixed nuts (your choice) - fruits of your choice INSTRUCTIONS: - Mix rolled oats and seeds add coconut water and water and bring to boil - while your porridge is simmering, add matcha powder and honey - when porridge is ready, put it in a bowl and top it with fruits (banana 🍌 in our case) and nuts, sprinkle with matcha powder and seeds mix.
