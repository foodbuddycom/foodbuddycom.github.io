Made my own broccoli falafel 😁 super delicious but time consuming 😬... better cook a big batch in one go ☝🏼You will need 1 big baked broccoli (cut into florets, mix with olive oil, salt and pepper, bake for 25 min at 200 C/392F) 3 cups of cooked chickpeas (garbanzo beans), 2 tbsp of lemon juice, 1 bunch of cilantro leaves, 1 tsp of parika, 1 tsp of chili flakes, 1 medium onion, 3-4 garlic cloves, olive oil ( stir fry garlic and onion until soft), 3/4 cup of wholemeal flour, salt pepper - BLEND IT ALL TOGETHER, set aside in fridge for 20 min, then make falafel balls and bake for 30 min at 200 C/ 392F turning around falafels after first 15 min.
