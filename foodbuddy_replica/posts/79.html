Here’s a great healthy waffles recipe for you! No refined sugar, no cows milk, no butter! Can they even be delicious?🤔 ABSOLUTELY! All you need is: ➖1 cup wholemeal flour ➖1 cup bread flour ➖4 tsp baking powder or 4 tsp of baking soda mixed with lemon juice (to remove strong taste of soda) ➖1/2 tsp salt ➖1 1/2 cup almond milk ➖2 eggs ➖2 tbsp sunflower oil ➖3 small bananas mashed ➖1 tbsp raw honey 🍯 ➖2 tbsp cocoa powder (optional, if you want to have chocolate waffles) ➖ 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) Mix well all ingredients into batter and pour a spoonful on your waffle iron, bake for few min until waffles are ready! 🧇 Add honey 🍯 and fruits 🍓🍌🍇🍊🥝 ENJOY! 😋 ___ #healthybreakfast #breakfastideas #healthywaffles #waffles #almondmilk #wafflesunday #cookathome #onmyplate #dairyfree #foodblogfeed #sgeats #foodsg #sgfoodie #feedfeed #theivemags #timeoutsg #buzzfeedfood #buzzfeedtasty #food52 #foodfluffer #foodbuddy #healthyrecipes
