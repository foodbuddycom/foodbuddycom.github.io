Good morning ☀️ one of the positive sides of working from home is more time to cook yummy breakfast in the morning 🧇😋 like these classic waffles 👌🏼 - 2 cups of bread flour - 2 tbsp brown sugar - 4 tsp baking powder - 1 tsp salt 🧂 - 2 eggs 🥚 - 1 1/2 to 2 cups of milk 🥛 - 1/3 cup of melted butter 🧈 - 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) Mix all dry ingredients, separately mix eggs, milk and butter. Combine fry and liquid mixtures into waffle batter. Ladle the batter into preheated waffle iron and cook until they are golden and crispy 👍🏼🧇 top up with favourite fruits, jam or sauce and enjoy! ___ #breakfastideas #itsalmostfriday #onmyplate #waffles #feedfeed #foodblogfeed #food52 #thrivemags #flatlaytoday #sgfoodie #foodsg #singapore #cookathome #homemadefood
