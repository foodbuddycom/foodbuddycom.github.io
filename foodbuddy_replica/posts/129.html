Hope you enjoy yours as we ours 🤗 If you need a recipe: ➖ 2 cups bread / plain flour ➖ 1.5 tbsp brown sugar ➖ 4 tsp baking powder ➖ 1/4 tsp baking soda (mix it with water before you add it to your batter) ➖4 tbsp of melted butter ➖1 large egg ➖1/2 tsp salt ➖1.5 cups of warm milk (and a bit extra to add in case your batter is too thick) ➖2 tsp vanilla extract (optional) Mix well all ingredients into batter that is thick but you still can pour it to a heated skillet, lightly greased with butter. When the underside is golden and bubbles begin to appear on the surface, flip your pancake and cook until golden.
