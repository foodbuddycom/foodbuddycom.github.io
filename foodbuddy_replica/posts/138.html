It doesn’t take 8 working hours to make this breakfast 😄 so don’t shy away and treat yourself to wholemeal banana chocolate waffles during long weekend 🧇😋 Here’s recipe: - 1 cup of wholemeal flour - 1.5 cup of milk 🥛 - 2 ripe bananas blended 🍌 - 2 tbsp cacao powder - 1 egg 🥚 - 2 tsp of baking powder - 1/2 tsp of salt 🧂 - vanilla extract (optional) Blend all ingredients into smooth batter and bake waffles.
