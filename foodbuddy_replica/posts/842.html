One of the most favourite Ottolenghi salads: beetroot, avocado, peas salad with mint, cilantro leaves, red onion and chilli, soya, olive oil and vinegar sauce - super delicious and healthy💪🏼😊! Beetroot are very rich in fiber, protein, potassium, manganese, and folic acid. Preparing this salad, thinly slice beetroots and blanch for 2-3 min (should be soft yet crunchy). Marinade onions in soya, chilli, vinegar and olive oil for 15 min, add salt and pepper to your taste!!! 😉
