Today we cooked this absolutely delicious curry lentil and potato soup 🍲 that was so good that we almost finished it all in one go 😬(cooking process is shared in stories 👆🏼) If you are looking for some yummy and easy vegan soup recipe you should definitely save this one: INGREDIENTS - 3 potatoes 🥔 cut into small cubes - 1 cup of green or brown lentils - 1 yellow onion 🧅 chopped into small cubes - 3 garlic 🧄cloves minced - 1 inch of fresh ginger minced - 1 can of diced tomatoes 🍅 - 1.5 cup of full fat coconut 🥥 milk - 2 tbsp of curry powder - 1 tsp of salt 🧂 - 1 tsp of cumin seeds - 0.5 tsp of cayenne pepper - cilantro leaves chopped 🌱 COOKING PROCESS - In a cooking pan bring potato to boil - Sauté onion, garlic and ginger and add them to boiling potato - add to the pot diced tomatoes 🍅, lentils, curry powder, cumin seeds, salt, cayenne pepper and simmer for 25 min - at the end add coconut milk to the soup and bring it to boil. - pour cooked soup to a bowl and add chopped cilantro leaves.
