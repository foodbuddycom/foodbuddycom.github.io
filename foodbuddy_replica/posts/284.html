To what extent?Multiple studies and researches were done to find out the effect of diary products on antioxidants, however their results are conflicting. Bottom line is that dairy products may reduce effect of antioxidants but not 100%.There is currently no evidence that consuming dairy with antioxidant-rich foods reduces their overall health benefits. We also need to keep in mind that many other factors affecting the antioxidant capacity (and even the nutrient content) of different foods.
