To continue on quinoa topic, here’s a great breakfast recipe for you that can be a great recovery meal after your morning workout 💪🏼 - quinoa porridge with seeds, honey, coconut milk, fruits and berries 😋 try it, I think you’ll like it 👍🏼! INGREDIENTS ➖1 cup quinoa (if time permits, soak it in water for 1 hr before you cook) ➖1/2 cup coconut milk 🥥 ➖ 1/2 tsp cinnamon ➖ 1 tbsp seeds mix (we had mix of flax, chia and hemp seeds) ➖ 1 tsp honey 🍯 ➖ fruits and berries of your choice COOKING METHOD: ➖cook quinoa as any other porridge, pour water (1.5 cm above quinoa) ➖when quinoa is semi-cooked, add coconut milk, seeds, and honey. Cook 5-10 min more until quinoa is soft. ➖top up your porridge with fruits and berries of your choice and enjoy! ___ #breakfastideas #healthybreakfast #quinoa #porridgebowl #quinoaporridge #recoverymeal #cookathome #feedfeed #foodblogfeed #buzzfeedfood #buzzfeedtasty #sgfoodie #foodsg #singapore #flatlaytoday
